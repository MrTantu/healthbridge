About HealthBridge

Bridging the gap between complex medical bills and financial peace of mind through AI technology.

Our Story

HealthBridge was born from a personal experience. Our founder watched a family member struggle with overwhelming medical bills after a major surgery. Despite having insurance, they faced thousands of dollars in unexpected charges and confusing paperwork.

We realized that millions of Americans face the same challenge every year. Medical billing errors cost patients billions annually, and most people lack the expertise to identify and dispute these errors.

That's when we decided to combine artificial intelligence with medical billing expertise to create a solution that empowers patients and saves them money.

Our Mission

To democratize access to medical bill auditing by making it fast, affordable, and accessible to everyone through AI technology.

Our Vision

A world where no one overpays for healthcare, and medical billing transparency is the standard, not the exception.
